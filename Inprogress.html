E-commerce Customer & Product Analytics

This project explores a comprehensive e-commerce dataset containing customer, product, transaction, and clickstream data. The goal is to uncover actionable insights about customer behavior, product performance, and business KPIs, and to showcase advanced data analytics skills.
  • Digging into Data

Dataset Description:

Customer Table: Detailed customer information including demographics, device type, and location.

Product Table: Fashion products with attributes like category, subcategory, article type, color, season, and usage.

Transaction Table: Every purchase made by a customer, including transaction amount, product metadata, payment status, and shipment details.

Clickstream Table: App usage events, session-level activity, and traffic source data.

Key Steps in the Project:


1. Data Processing

Merging customer, product, and transaction tables to create a unified dataset.

Extracting relevant features from dictionary-type metadata (product_metadata, event_metadata) for analysis.

Handling missing values, ensured proper data types for categorical and datetime fields.

Creating derived features such as recency, frequency, and monetary values for each customer.

2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

2.1 Customer Insights:

Gender distribution, geographical spread, and device usage trends.

Customer acquisition over time and loyalty patterns.

2.2 Transaction Insights:

Daily orders and revenue trends.

Payment methods, promotions, and shipment insights.

2.3 Product Insights:

Top-selling products and categories.

Revenue and order distribution by master category, subcategory, and article type.

2.4 Clickstream Insights:

Session duration, number of events, and traffic source analysis.

3. Deep Analysis & Metrics

3.1 RFM Analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary):

Segment customers based on recency of purchase, number of transactions, and total spending.

Identify loyal, at-risk, and new customers for targeted marketing.

3.2 Top Products & Categories:

Ranking products and categories by sales and order count.

Seasonal trends and color/popularity patterns.

3.3 Customer Behavior & Engagement:

Clickstream analysis for conversion funnel insights.

Device type and traffic source impact on purchase behavior.

3.4 Business Metrics:

Total revenue, average order value, repeat purchase rate.

Insights into high-performing product lines and underperforming segments.

4. Dashboarding Ideas

Key dashboards include:

Sales Dashboard: Revenue trends, top products, category performance, geographic distribution.

Customer Dashboard: RFM segments, loyalty metrics, demographic insights.

Product Dashboard: Product sales ranking, seasonal trends, subcategory performance.

Clickstream Dashboard: Session analysis, funnel visualization, engagement metrics.

5. Key Takeaways

Identifying High-performing products and categories, to optimize inventory and marketing.

RFM segmentation for highlighting opportunities to retain at-risk customers and encourage repeat purchases.

Seasonal and color-based product trends for planning future product launches.

Clickstream analysis for getting insights into the customer journey, showing areas to improve app engagement and conversion.

6. Project Highlights

Application of advanced data preprocessing and feature extraction from nested metadata.

Conducting comprehensive EDA and deriving actionable business insights.

Creating RFM-based customer segmentation for strategic marketing decisions.

Demonstrating full end-to-end analytics workflow from raw data to insights.
