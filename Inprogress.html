Analyzing Local Business & Service Reviews Across U.S. Cities

This project aims to analyze local business reviews and operational data across multiple cities in the United States, combining structured information from business listings (e.g., hours, services, categories) with unstructured customer reviews (ratings, comments, and responses).
  • Digging into data

The goal is to create insights that are useful for:



🔹Business owners (to understand strengths, weaknesses, and competitive positioning).

🔹Consumers (to make informed decisions about where to shop, dine, or receive services).

🔹Researchers & policymakers (to examine how regional differences influence service quality, accessibility, and customer trust).

Data Sources:

🔹Google Maps Business Listings: Structured data such as business name, category, location (latitude/longitude), hours, service options, accessibility features, and average ratings.

🔹Customer Reviews: User-submitted reviews with text, ratings, images, and business responses.

🔹Geographic Scope: Multiple cities across the U.S. (urban, suburban, and rural coverage) to ensure diversity in business environments.

By bringing together diverse data sources such as pharmacies, dental offices, restaurants, and recreational facilities, we will uncover patterns in customer satisfaction, service quality, accessibility, and regional business practices.

Example Data:


1. Healthcare & Dentistry

🔹Example: Review of a dental office in Indiana, with customer comments, star rating, and a direct response from the provider.

🔹Features: High-quality customer feedback with sentiment-rich text and service personalization.

2. Pharmacies (Retail Chains)

🔹Example: Walgreens Pharmacy with business attributes like hours, health & safety policies, service options (drive-through, curbside pickup), accessibility, and payment methods.

🔹Features: Business compliance, operational data, and customer expectations for health-related services.

Methodology:


1. Data Collection & Integration

🔹Aggregate business and review datasets from multiple U.S. cities.

🔹Standardize fields such as categories, hours, and service options.

2. Text Analytics & Sentiment Analysis

🔹Extract customer sentiments, recurring themes, and pain points from review text.

🔹Compare sentiment with star ratings to detect mismatches.

3. Geospatial & Regional Comparison

🔹Map business data to city and neighborhood levels.

🔹Identify regional trends (e.g., Midwest vs. West Coast differences in accessibility or service quality).

4. Business Response Analysis

🔹Track how often businesses respond to reviews and how response quality correlates with overall rating improvement.

Potential Insights:

🔹Customer Priorities by Region: Do urban customers care more about convenience while rural customers emphasize personal service?

🔹Impact of Safety & Accessibility: Are pharmacies with stricter health/safety protocols rated higher post-COVID?

🔹Role of Business Responses: Do dentist offices with personalized responses build stronger trust and higher ratings?

🔹Category-Specific Trends: How do pharmacies, healthcare providers, and other essential services compare in ratings and feedback?

Deliverables:

🔹Interactive Dashboards (in Looker Studio) showing review sentiment trends, rating distributions, and geographic heatmaps.

🔹Analytical Report summarizing key findings across cities.

🔹Case Studies highlighting standout businesses or regions with unique practices.

This project will serve as a benchmark for understanding how local businesses operate and engage with customers across the U.S., revealing actionable insights for businesses, consumers, and researchers alike.
