Loan Data Analysis & Default Risk Insights

I’m gearing up to dive into a comprehensive analysis of a large-scale loan dataset, designed to uncover patterns in lending, borrower behavior, and repayment performance. This project will explore how borrower demographics, credit history, and loan characteristics influence default risk, providing actionable insights for smarter lending decisions.
  • Digging into Data

What’s Coming:

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):

Visualizing distributions of loan amounts, interest rates, and borrower demographics. Identifying trends, outliers, and patterns in repayment behavior.

Credit & Risk Analysis:

Deep dive into credit utilization, debt-to-income ratios, and the factors driving defaults.

Temporal Trends:

Tracking loan issuance and repayment trends over time to understand evolving borrower behavior.

Feature Engineering:

Creating new metrics like credit age, income-to-loan ratio, and late payment flags to highlight risk indicators.

Predictive Modeling (Planned):

Predicting default probability using machine learning, identifying key features that influence risk.

Actionable Insights:

Recommending strategies to optimize lending, mitigate risk, and better serve borrowers.

Why It Matters:

With growing volumes of peer-to-peer and online lending, understanding the risk landscape is crucial. This project aims to translate complex data into clear, actionable insights, helping financial institutions make smarter, data-driven lending decisions.

Tech Stack & Tools (Planned):

Data Analysis:

Python (Pyspark, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly)

Data Storage & Querying:

Google BigQuery

Big Data & Cloud Computing:

Databricks for large-scale data processing

Machine Learning:

Scikit-learn, XGBoost

Portfolio Presentation:

Jupyter Notebook + interactive visualizations

Sneak Peek at Insights (Coming Soon):

How loan grade and interest rates impact default risk.

Key borrower characteristics that correlate with repayment success.

Regional and temporal patterns in lending and defaults.

E-commerce Customer & Product Insights

Working on a comprehensive E-commerce Project that will unify customer behavior, product performance, and transactional analytics. This project will leverage clickstream data, transaction history, and detailed product and customer metadata to provide actionable insights. Key initiatives include customer segmentation using RFM analysis, cohort and retention studies, basket analysis for product affinities, predictive modeling for churn and conversion.
  • Digging into Data

Why It Matters:

Understanding customer behavior and product performance is essential for optimizing sales, marketing strategies, and user experience. By extracting actionable insights from multiple data sources, this project will enable:

Targeted marketing campaigns

Based on customer segments and predicted lifetime value.

Improved product recommendations

And personalized experiences, increasing conversion rates.

Operational efficiency

By identifying high-performing products and sales trends.

Strategic decision-making

With dashboards and predictive analytics to anticipate churn, forecast revenue, and optimize inventory.

Tech Stack & Tools:

Data Engineering & Storage:

Python (pandas, numpy), SQL, BigQuery / Databricks for scalable data processing.

Data Analysis & Visualization:

Matplotlib, Plotly, Seaborn, Jupyter Notebooks, Power BI or Data Studio dashboards.

Feature Engineering & Machine Learning:

scikit-learn, lifetimes (CLTV), mlxtend (basket analysis), Random Forests, Gradient Boosting, and cohort analysis frameworks.

Dashboard & Delivery:

Streamlit for interactive dashboards, with optional integration into Databricks for collaborative exploration.

Sneak Peek at Insights (Coming soon):

Customer Segments:

Identification of high-value customers with frequent purchases and high average order values, along with at-risk churn segments.

Cohort & Retention Patterns:

Insight into how customer retention evolves over time and which cohorts are most engaged.

Top Products & Categories:

Early analysis reveals which products and categories drive the most revenue and repeat purchases.

Conversion & Funnel Metrics:

Analysis of session activity and device usage to pinpoint conversion bottlenecks and optimize UX.

Product Affinities:

Initial basket analysis highlights frequently bought-together items, setting the stage for smarter cross-selling strategies.

This project combines deep data analysis with predictive modeling to transform raw e-commerce activity into strategic insights, enabling smarter decisions and measurable business impact.

My Data-to-Decision Workflow

Think of my projects as a journey: data is the raw map, tools like SQL, Python, and Power BI are my compass, and the destination is always one thing—clear, actionable insights that empower smarter business moves.

  • Problem Understanding

    Start by identifying the core business question or challenge, making sure I understand the “why” behind the project.

  • Data Collection & Preparation

    Gather, clean, and transform raw data into a reliable dataset (SQL, Python, Excel, or APIs).

  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

    Dive into the data to uncover patterns, anomalies, and key relationships.

  • Modeling & Analysis

    Apply statistical methods, machine learning, or business logic to generate meaningful insights.

  • Visualization & Storytelling

    Build clear, interactive dashboards (Power BI/Tableau) or visualizations to communicate findings effectively.

  • Documentation & Knowledge Sharing

    Document methods, create reports, and make sure results are reproducible and actionable.
