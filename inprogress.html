Databricks (Notebooks, Delta Tables, Auto-Loader, Jobs, MLflow), Apache Spark for distributed data processing, Python / PySpark / Pandas for transformations, scikit-learn & XGBoost for ML modeling, MLflow for experiment tracking and model registry, BigQuery for Data Storage

My Data-to-Decision Workflow

Think of my projects as a journey: data is the raw map, tools like SQL, Python, and Power BI are my compass, and the destination is always one thing—clear, actionable insights that empower smarter business moves.

  • Problem Understanding

    Start by identifying the core business question or challenge, making sure I understand the “why” behind the project.

  • Data Collection & Preparation

    Gather, clean, and transform raw data into a reliable dataset (SQL, Python, Excel, or APIs).

  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

    Dive into the data to uncover patterns, anomalies, and key relationships.

  • Modeling & Analysis

    Apply statistical methods, machine learning, or business logic to generate meaningful insights.

  • Visualization & Storytelling

    Build clear, interactive dashboards (Power BI/Tableau) or visualizations to communicate findings effectively.

  • Documentation & Knowledge Sharing

    Document methods, create reports, and make sure results are reproducible and actionable.
