Loan Data Analysis & Default Risk Insights

Deep diving into a comprehensive analysis of a large-scale loan dataset, designed to uncover patterns in lending, borrower behavior, and repayment performance. This project will explore how borrower demographics, credit history, and loan characteristics influence default risk, providing actionable insights for smarter lending decisions.
  • Shaping up

Key Objectives:

1. Understanding loan distribution and borrower demographics.

2. Exploring correlations between loan characteristics and default risk.

3. Identify patterns in credit behavior, repayment, and financial health.

4.Provide recommendations for lending strategies and risk mitigation.

What’s Coming:

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):

Visualizing distributions of loan amounts, interest rates, and borrower demographics. Identifying trends, outliers, and patterns in repayment behavior.

Credit & Risk Analysis:

Deep dive into credit utilization, debt-to-income ratios, and the factors driving defaults.

Temporal Trends:

Tracking loan issuance and repayment trends over time to understand evolving borrower behavior.

Feature Engineering:

Creating new metrics like credit age, income-to-loan ratio, and late payment flags to highlight risk indicators.

Predictive Modeling (Planned):

Predicting default probability using machine learning, identifying key features that influence risk.

Actionable Insights:

Recommending strategies to optimize lending, mitigate risk, and better serve borrowers.

Why It Matters:

With growing volumes of peer-to-peer and online lending, understanding the risk landscape is crucial. This project aims to translate complex data into clear, actionable insights, helping financial institutions make smarter, data-driven lending decisions.

Tech Stack & Tools (Planned):

Data Analysis:

Python (Pyspark, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly)

Data Storage & Querying:

Google BigQuery

Big Data & Cloud Computing:

Databricks for large-scale data processing

Machine Learning:

Scikit-learn, XGBoost

Portfolio Presentation:

Jupyter Notebook + interactive visualizations

Sneak Peek at Insights (Coming Soon):

How loan grade and interest rates impact default risk.

Key borrower characteristics that correlate with repayment success.

Regional and temporal patterns in lending and defaults.
